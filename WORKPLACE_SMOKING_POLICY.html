Smoking Policy at Workplace (WORKPLACE_SMOKING_POLICY)

Describes the smoking policy at the participant's current workplace, ranging from no policy to a policy that limits or prohibits smoking; coded as not applicable for those who do not work full-time or part-time in an indoor office environment around others.
