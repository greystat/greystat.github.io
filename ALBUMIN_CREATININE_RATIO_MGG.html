Urinary Albumin/Creatinine Ratio (mg/g) (ALBUMIN_CREATININE_RATIO_MGG)

Urinary albumin-to-creatinine ratio in mg/g, calculated as 1000 × (urinary albumin / urinary creatinine). This ratio is used to assess albuminuria and kidney function.
