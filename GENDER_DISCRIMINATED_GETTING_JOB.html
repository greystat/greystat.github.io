Gender Discrimination Getting a Job Frequency (GENDER_DISCRIMINATED_GETTING_JOB)

Among participants who reported ever experiencing gender discrimination when getting a job, the frequency of such discrimination (rarely, sometimes, or often). Assessed by asking whether the participant ever experienced discrimination, was prevented from doing something, or was hassled or made to feel inferior when getting a job because of their gender.
